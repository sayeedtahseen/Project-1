# Project-1 - Analysis of Seattle AirBnb

## Motivation for Project:
Analyze Seattle Airbnb Market to find criteria's influencing the price of listings, which includes bedrooms, bathrooms, time of the year, neighbourhood listing is located at, etc.

## Summary:
There are external factors affecting the price of a listing other than the number of bedrooms, bathrooms, number of people it accomodates and such. The time of the year when a listing is being booked plays a part, as the demand for AirBnb rises during the summer and winter seasons. The neighborhoods in which a listing is located in also influences the price.

## Libraries Used:
